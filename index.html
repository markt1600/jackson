The Drip
Atelier ·

An algorithmic studio in the manner of Jackson Pollock. Each pour is one of a kind — flung, dripped and pooled in enamel, then hung for you at archival resolution.

Each fresh painting is thrown over about three seconds — physics-simulated pours under real gravity. Renders at up to 4,400 px on the long edge; downloads include your chosen frame. No two pours repeat.
